📅 30-Day Learning Plan

By Day 30, you’ll understand digital marketing basics, SEO, content writing, and have started building a small portfolio.

📍 Week 1 (Days 1–7) – Understand the Basics

  • Day 1: Intro to Digital Marketing – Google Digital Garage
  • Day 2: Continue Course (Modules 2–3) – Online Opportunity, Business Strategy
  • Day 3: Continue Course (Modules 4–5) – Digital Presence, Website Basics
  • Day 4: What is SEO + Why it Matters – HubSpot SEO Course
  • Day 5: On-Page SEO Basics – On-page optimization, URL, meta tags
  • Day 6: Keywords & Meta Tags – Keyword research module
  • Day 7: Recap + Quiz – Google Digital Garage quiz + your own notes

📍 Week 2 (Days 8–14) – Content Writing & Tools

  • Day 8: Basics of Blog Writing – HubSpot Content Marketing Course
  • Day 9: Structure a Blog Post – HubSpot Lessons 4–6
  • Day 10: Write Your 1st Blog Draft – Use your favorite topic or niche
  • Day 11: Learn Grammarly + Google Docs – Grammarly, Google Docs
  • Day 12: Edit Blog + Add SEO Keywords – Use Ubersuggest for keywords
  • Day 13: Create Canva Account + Try a Social Post – Canva
  • Day 14: Design 1–2 Social Media Graphics – Based on your blog or a quote you like

📍 Week 3 (Days 15–21) – Start Building Your Portfolio

  • Day 15: Set up a WordPress Blog – WordPress
  • Day 16: Upload Your 1st Blog Post – Use title, featured image, SEO tags
  • Day 17: Write a 2nd Blog Post – Pick a topic relevant to digital trends
  • Day 18: Design 3 More Canva Posts – Match your blog’s color palette
  • Day 19: Learn Internal Linking + SEO Tools – Install RankMath or Yoast plugin
  • Day 20: Add Portfolio Page to Blog – List your blog links + designs
  • Day 21: Review Portfolio + Polish Writing – Get feedback from peers or Grammarly

📍 Week 4 (Days 22–30) – Prepare to Apply + Freelance

  • Day 22: Create Your Resume (Content Focused) – Zety Resume Builder
  • Day 23: Set Up Fiverr + LinkedIn Profile – Fiverr, LinkedIn
  • Day 24: Post 1 Gig: “SEO Blog Writing” – Fiverr – Add samples from blog
  • Day 25: Apply for 2–3 Internships – Internshala
  • Day 26: Create 1 More Blog or SEO Article – Use HubSpot or Neil Patel SEO checklist
  • Day 27: Connect with 5 LinkedIn Professionals – Search for digital marketers or bloggers
  • Day 28: Ask for Feedback on Your Portfolio – Online communities or local mentors
  • Day 29: Apply to 2 Freelance Projects – Try Upwork or Fiverr gigs
  • Day 30: Reflect & Plan Next Month – Plan next niche: email marketing, ads, etc.

⏳ Progress Monitoring

  • Use Google Sheets Progress Tracker or Notion to track each day's task.
  • Assign 1–5 star rating to each day’s task after completion.
  • Weekly summary to visualize progress (Graph: Task Completed vs. Time Spent).
